Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How long does it take to process my request?

Most academic service requests are processed within 3-5 business days. Urgent requests may be expedited.

Can I cancel my request after submission?

Yes, you can cancel pending requests from your requests page. Once processing begins, cancellation may not be possible.

How do I download my documents?

Visit your Document Inbox to view and download all available documents. You'll receive an email notification when new documents are ready.

What if I need urgent assistance?

For urgent matters, please call our support line at +1 (555) 123-4567 or visit the Student Services Office in person.
